About Us

Genelibs.com is a website that offers access to gene expression data and analysis tools for researchers and scientists. The Gene Browser page provides a user-friendly interface to browse and search gene expression data from different tissues and cell types, such as liver, brain, heart, and more. The website also offers access to pre-calculated differential gene expression analysis results based on RNA-Seq data from multiple studies.

Our Company

Jinan Honghui Biotechnology Co., Ltd. is a company based in Jinan, China, specializing in the research, development, and production of various bioinformatics tools. The company provides bioinformatics software, data analysis services, and customized solutions for researchers and organizations working in areas such as drug discovery, personalized medicine, and agricultural biotechnology.
