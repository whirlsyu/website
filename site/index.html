About Genelibs

Genelibs is a next-generation bioinformatics platform that integrates big data analysis with AI algorithms to revolutionize gene research. We empower researchers worldwide with cutting-edge tools for data mining, analysis, and visualization.

Our Mission

To address the common challenge of researchers lacking programming expertise while needing to leverage the latest gene databases, we've developed an innovative analysis platform. By combining big data analytics with gene technology and integrating major international public databases, we enable scientists to harness database resources effectively for data mining - truly transforming traditional research methodologies.

  Core Technology

We leverage AlphaFold's triangular self-attention mechanism, Transformer's global modeling capabilities, and diffusion model frameworks for comprehensive multi-omics analysis.

  Data Integration

Our platform seamlessly integrates GEO, TCGA, and other public databases, enabling researchers to perform comprehensive data mining across multiple sources.

  Community Driven

Join over 150 researchers and developers contributing to our open-source algorithms and collaborative projects.
